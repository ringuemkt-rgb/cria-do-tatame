# Cria do Tatame — Prompts de Personagens Pixel Art

## Direcao global

Estilo: HD Pixel Art 2.5D Regional Premium, mobile-first, leitura de silhueta em 1 segundo, sem marcas reais, sem atletas reais, sem copiar obras existentes.

Formato recomendado:

- PNG transparente.
- Frame minimo: 256x256.
- Pivot: base dos pes.
- Downscale: gerar grande e reduzir com nearest neighbor.
- Paleta base: preto fosco, dourado queimado, branco sujo, azul rio, vermelho crise.

Negative prompt:

```text
real athlete, real logo, real brand, blurry, low quality, distorted anatomy, extra limbs, unreadable silhouette, plastic skin, excessive gore, firearm, medieval fantasy armor, sci-fi armor, random text, watermark
```

## Acoes de Ruan

Use o prompt mestre e acrescente uma linha por acao:

- idle: `standing relaxed but alert, heavy base, hands ready, calm breathing`
- walk: `walking cycle pose, grounded footwork, jiu-jitsu athlete movement`
- stance: `combat stance, knees bent, hands ready for grip fight`
- grip: `attempting gi grip, one hand reaching lapel, other hand guarding`
- clinch: `collar tie and sleeve control stance, safe sport grappling pose`
- takedown: `entering safe sport takedown pose, dynamic low level change`
- guard: `closed guard defensive position, sport jiu-jitsu framing, safe visual`
- pass: `guard passing posture, knee cut inspired movement, controlled pressure`
- mount: `dominant mount control, stable hips, sport jiu-jitsu control`
- submission_setup: `technical submission setup, controlled safe sport grappling, no injury focus`
- submission_lock: `submission control phase, tap-ready sport context, referee-safe visual`
- submission_finish: `technical finish moment, opponent tapping, respect and safety`
- win: `humble victory pose, tired breathing, no arrogance`
- lose: `sitting on mat after defeat, ashamed but alive, emotional weight`
